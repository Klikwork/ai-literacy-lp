# Landing Page Briefing
## AI Literacy for Recruiters — Certificate Training by Klikwork

**Prepared for:** AI Studio website builder
**Page URL:** ailiteracyforrecruiters.com
**Goal:** Convert visitors into buyers of the AI Literacy for Recruiters training
**Date:** April 2026

---

## 1. Context

Klikwork BV is a Dutch AI recruitment training company.

This landing page promotes the AI Literacy for Recruiters online training: a 30-minute self-paced program across three modules with a knowledge test and a digital certificate on passing. The certificate demonstrates compliance with Article 4 of the EU AI Act.

---

## 2. Brand guidelines

**Colors:**
- Background: `#0b0e14` (near black, dark mode)
- Primary text: `#ffffff` (white)
- Accent: `#ff5500` (Klikwork orange, use sparingly, one accent per section)
- Secondary text: `#9ca3af` (mid gray)
- Card backgrounds: `#161b26`

**Typography:**
- Font: Roboto (Google Fonts). Bold for headings, regular for body.
- Fallback: Helvetica, system-ui, Arial, sans-serif

**Logo:** Use the white Klikwork logo (K icon + KLIKWORK wordmark) on dark backgrounds. Never use on light backgrounds.

**Tone:** Calm. Direct. Human. No hype. Write like a practitioner, not a vendor.
- Banned words: revolutionary, game-changing, cutting-edge, unlock, leverage, disrupt, supercharge, transform (as hype verb)
- Short sentences. Active voice. No filler.
- Show outcomes, not promises.

**Imagery:** No robots, no brains, no magic wand metaphors. If imagery is used, show recruiters at work, screens with real workflows, or simple icons. Abstract geometric shapes are fine.

---

## 3. Page goal and single action

**One goal:** Get the visitor to click Buy and complete payment via Plug and Pay.

There is no newsletter signup, no lead magnet, no secondary CTA. The page exists to sell one thing.

**Primary CTA button:** "Start the training" or "Get certified"
**Button color:** `#ff5500`

---

## 4. Target audience

- Individual recruiters who want to demonstrate AI literacy to their employer
- HR managers looking for a compliant, certifiable training for their team
- Recruitment agencies that need to document AI literacy under the EU AI Act
- Corporate TA teams buying multiple licenses for their recruiters

### SECTION 7: FAQ

Six questions, accordion style.

**How long does the training take?**
30 minutes total across three modules. You can complete it in one sitting or spread it across multiple sessions.

**What happens after I pay?**
You receive an email within a few minutes with your personal access link. Click it and you can start immediately. No account creation required.

**What score do I need to pass?**
8 out of 10 on the knowledge test. If you do not pass the first time, you can retry as many times as you need. There is no penalty for retrying.

**Is the certificate recognised?**
The certificate demonstrates AI literacy as required under Article 4 of the EU AI Act. It is issued by Klikwork BV and includes a public verification link you can share with your employer. It is valid for one year.

**Can I buy licenses for my whole team?**
Yes. Purchase the tier that matches your team size. You will automatically receive a dashboard where you can invite your team members. Each person gets their own magic link, completes the training independently, and receives their own certificate on passing.

**Can I do the training on my phone?**
Yes. The training works on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile. Watch the modules and complete the test wherever it suits you.

**Are subtitles available?**
Yes. All three video modules include subtitles so you can follow along in any environment, with or without sound.

**In what language is the training?**
English. The training is designed for international recruitment professionals and uses straightforward business English throughout.
