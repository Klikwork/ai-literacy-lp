# Klikwork Service Portfolio

This is the canonical reference for all Klikwork offerings. Other skills should read this file rather than maintaining their own version.

## Training Programs (for teams and organizations)

| Track | Format | Duration | Target Group | AI Maturity |
|-------|--------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| Foundation | Online program | Self-paced (~4 weeks) | Recruiters with low AI literacy | Level 0-1 |
| Pro | Online program | Self-paced (~6 weeks) | Recruiters ready to build workflows | Level 1-2 |
| Elite | Online + live coaching | ~8 weeks | Senior recruiters / leads who want to become internal AI champions | Level 2-3 |
| In-house Bootcamp | On-site, intensive | 1-2 days | Teams who want hands-on acceleration | Any level |
